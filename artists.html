Magnolia Springs Harvest Arts Festival

October 18-20, 2024 | Magnolia Springs, Georgia

Music, Food, Art, and Autumn Fun

Join us for a weekend of live music, handmade art, local food, and community at the Magnolia Springs Harvest Arts Festival. Celebrate the fall season with talented performers, artists, makers, and food vendors from across Georgia.

Event Location and Hours

Location: Riverside Commons, 101 Magnolia Way, Magnolia Springs, Georgia

  • Friday, October 18: 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM
  • Saturday, October 19: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM
  • Sunday, October 20: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

2024 Featured Artists

Meet the musicians performing throughout the festival weekend.

The Juniper Row

Genre: Americana and Folk

The Juniper Row is a three-person folk group known for acoustic guitar, rich harmonies, and songs inspired by Southern back roads.

Performance: Friday, October 18 at 6:00 PM

Marisol Vale

Genre: Soul and R&B

Marisol Vale combines soulful vocals, heartfelt lyrics, and modern rhythm and blues into an unforgettable live performance.

Performance: Friday, October 18 at 8:15 PM

Peachtree Brass Co.

Genre: Brass, Funk, and Jazz

This nine-piece Georgia band brings energetic horn arrangements, funk rhythms, and plenty of crowd participation.

Performance: Saturday, October 19 at 12:30 PM

River & the Pines

Genre: Indie Rock

River & the Pines creates upbeat indie rock with bright guitar sounds, catchy choruses, and lyrics about small-town life.

Performance: Saturday, October 19 at 3:30 PM

DJ Sunny Side

Genre: Dance and Afrobeat

DJ Sunny Side mixes global dance music, Afrobeat, pop, and feel-good classics for a high-energy sunset set.

Performance: Saturday, October 19 at 6:45 PM

Hollow Creek Revival

Genre: Bluegrass and Roots Music

Hollow Creek Revival closes out the festival with banjo-driven songs, fiddle melodies, and classic bluegrass energy.

Performance: Sunday, October 20 at 3:30 PM

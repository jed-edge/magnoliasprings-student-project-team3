Vendor & Artist Registration

Join us for Magnolia Springs! Fill out the form below to apply as a vendor or artist. Our team reviews every submission and will follow up by email within 5–7 business days.

Registration Form

Before You Apply

  • Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, so early submissions are encouraged.
  • A confirmation email will be sent once your spot is secured.
  • Booth fees are due within 10 days of acceptance.
  • All vendors must provide their own tables, tents, and signage.
  • Food vendors must include a valid health permit number in their application.

Questions? Reach out to us through any of the social links in the footer below, and a member of our team will get back to you.
